What We Do

As three women interested in technology and starting our journey at Girls Who Code, we would like to make other women more aware of the change in gender gap of technology. We explain some of the different areas in tech and provide jobs for our users to apply to. We want our users to be able to network with others, so we provide a networking page whereby we list important people in tech and allow our users to contact them via LinkedIn. Another one of our page is called Women in Tech, and this is where we want to raise awareness of the need for women in these fields. The future is technology, and we want to educate others on it, so tech us out.
